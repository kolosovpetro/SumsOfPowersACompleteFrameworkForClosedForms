The main results establish an algorithm~\eqref{alg:sums-of-powers}
for deriving closed formulas for multifold sums of powers of integers
by combining variations of Newton's interpolation formula with
hockey-stick family identities for binomial coefficients.
Using the algorithm, some remarkable formulas were obtained,
closed formula for sums of powers~\eqref{prop:ordinary-sums-of-powers-decomposition},
formula for $r-$fold sums of powers~\eqref{thm:multifold-sums-of-powers}, formula
for $r-$fold sums of powers in pure binomial view~\eqref{prop:multifold-binomial-sums-of-powers}.
In addition, we have shown that famous Knuth's formula for sums of odd powers~\eqref{prop:knuth-odd-powers-sum}
originates from Newton's interpolation formula~\eqref{lem:central-newtons-formula-for-powers},
thus generalizing it for $r-$fold case~\eqref{prop:knuth-odd-powers-sum-multifold}.
